1. Bespoke & Custom Orders

When you commission a bespoke or custom garment from StitchCraft, you are engaging in a highly personalized process:

  • Consultation & Measurements: All custom orders begin with a detailed consultation and measurement session. We request that you wear well-fitting undergarments and appropriate footwear to ensure accuracy.
  • Fabric Selection: Once a fabric is cut for a custom order, it cannot be reused. Therefore, the cost of the fabric is non-refundable once production has commenced.
  • Fittings: Bespoke tailoring requires multiple fittings (typically 2-4). It is crucial that you attend these fittings promptly to avoid delays in final delivery.
  • Weight Fluctuations: StitchCraft is not responsible for fit issues arising from significant weight loss or gain between the initial measurement and the final fitting.

2. Alterations Policy

For garments brought in for alterations:

  • Assessment: Our tailors will assess the garment and provide a quote before any work begins.
  • Limitations: While we strive for perfection, some fabrics or previous alterations may limit what can be achieved. We will communicate any risks prior to beginning work.
  • Uncollected Items: Garments left uncollected for more than 90 days after the agreed completion date may be donated to charity, unless prior arrangements have been made.

3. Payment Terms

We accept major credit cards, debit cards, and bank transfers.

  • Custom Garments: A 50% non-refundable deposit is required before any cutting or sewing begins. The remaining 50% balance is due upon final collection of the garment.
  • Alterations: Payment in full is typically required at the time of drop-off, unless otherwise agreed upon.

4. Refunds & Returns

Due to the highly personalized nature of our business, our refund policy is strictly limited:
