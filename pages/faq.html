Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our tailoring services, bespoke process, and alterations.

General Tailoring

What types of garments do you tailor?
We tailor a wide variety of garments including suits, dresses, coats, trousers, blouses, and evening wear. We work with all types of fabrics, from delicate silks and laces to heavy wools and leathers, ensuring a perfect fit for any item in your wardrobe.
How long does a standard alteration take?
Standard alterations typically take between 3 to 7 business days, depending on the complexity of the work and our current schedule. During peak seasons, it may take slightly longer. We always provide an estimated completion date when you drop off your garment.
Do I need an appointment for a fitting?
While walk-ins are welcome for simple alterations like hemming, we highly recommend booking an appointment for complex alterations, bespoke services, or bridal wear. This ensures our master tailors can dedicate uninterrupted time to you.
Can you replicate an existing piece of clothing?
Yes, we specialize in garment replication. If you have a favorite piece of clothing that fits perfectly but is worn out, we can carefully study its pattern and create a flawless replica using new, high-quality fabrics of your choice.
What should I bring to my fitting appointment?
For the most accurate fit, please bring the exact undergarments and shoes you plan to wear with the garment. The height of your heel and the structure of your undergarments significantly affect how the clothing drapes and where hems should fall.

Bespoke & Custom

What is the difference between made-to-measure and bespoke?
Made-to-measure takes a pre-existing pattern and modifies it to your measurements. Bespoke is an entirely custom process where a unique pattern is drafted from scratch exclusively for your body shape and posture, offering unparalleled fit and personalization.
How many fittings are required for a bespoke suit?
A true bespoke suit typically requires three to four fittings. The first is a skeleton baste fitting, followed by a forward fitting, and finally a finishing fitting. This iterative process ensures the garment is sculpted perfectly to your physique.
Do you provide the fabrics, or can I bring my own?
We offer an extensive, curated selection of premium fabrics from world-renowned mills. However, you are more than welcome to bring your own fabric. Our tailors will assess it to ensure it is suitable for the design you have in mind.
How long does it take to create a custom garment from scratch?
A bespoke garment typically takes 4 to 8 weeks from the initial consultation to the final delivery, depending on the complexity of the design, fabric availability, and the number of required fittings.
Can you design a garment based on a picture I provide?
Absolutely. Reference photos are a fantastic starting point. Our designers will work with you to understand the elements you love about the picture and adapt the design to suit your body type and personal style preferences.

Alterations & Repairs

Can you alter clothing that is several sizes too big or too small?
Sizing down is generally easier and we can often reduce a garment by 2-3 sizes without ruining its proportions. Sizing up depends entirely on whether there is enough extra fabric (seam allowance) left inside the garment by the manufacturer.
Do you offer repair services for damaged garments?
Yes, we offer comprehensive repair services including zipper replacement, button reattachment, tear mending, relining jackets and coats, and re-weaving small holes. We aim to make repairs as invisible as possible.
Can you alter leather or suede garments?
Yes, we have specialized equipment and experienced artisans who are highly skilled in altering and repairing leather and suede garments. These materials require special handling, and we take the utmost care with them.
What is the average cost for hemming a dress or pants?
The cost of hemming varies based on the garment's construction. A simple pant hem starts around $20, while a multi-layered evening gown or a pair of lined trousers with a cuff will cost more. We provide a transparent quote before any work begins.
Do you offer express or same-day alteration services?
We do offer express and rush services for an additional fee, subject to our tailors' availability. Same-day service is sometimes possible for simple jobs like hemming or button replacement, provided you come in early in the day.
